# TimeKind App Store Descriptions

## iOS App Store

### App Name
TimeKind

### Subtitle
Understand Your Time, Build Better Habits

### Description

Time feels different for everyone. TimeKind is a calm, data-driven companion app that helps you notice patterns in how you estimate time versus how long tasks actually take.

**Why TimeKind?**

If you're neurodivergent (ADHD, autism, dyslexia), time perception can be tricky. You might estimate a task will take 30 minutes, but it actually takes 2 hours—or vice versa. TimeKind helps you build better time intuition through gentle tracking and visual insights.

**Core Features**

- **Task Timer**: Start a task, pause when needed, see how long it actually took
- **Accuracy Insights**: Track the gap between your estimates and reality over 7 and 30-day periods
- **Productivity Heatmap**: Visualize when you work best—by day of week and time of day
- **Energy Tracking**: Log your energy level with each task to spot patterns
- **Breathing Reset**: 30-second guided breathing animation when you need a mental break
- **Task Templates**: Pre-configured routines for morning, workouts, creative work, and deep focus
- **Mood Tracking**: Rate your mood (1-5 stars) after completing tasks to correlate with productivity
- **Smart Scheduling**: Get recommendations based on your energy patterns and historical accuracy
- **Offline First**: All data stays on your device—no internet required, complete privacy
- **Accessible Design**: Large, readable text; optional animations, sounds, and notifications; keyboard shortcuts for power users
- **Weekly Reflection**: AI-powered summary of your week's patterns and insights

**Neurodivergent-Friendly**

- Large, readable typography throughout
- All sensory features (animations, sounds, notifications) are toggleable
- No overwhelming visuals or gamification—just calm, honest data
- Gentle notifications that respect your preferences
- Designed for one-handed mobile use

**Privacy First**

Your data never leaves your device. TimeKind is completely offline and doesn't require an account, cloud sync, or internet connection. You're in control.

**Get Started**

1. Create a task with your time estimate
2. Start the timer and work
3. Complete the task and rate your accuracy
4. Watch your patterns emerge over time
5. Use insights to build better time intuition

TimeKind is for anyone who wants to understand their relationship with time—especially neurodivergent users building better habits.
