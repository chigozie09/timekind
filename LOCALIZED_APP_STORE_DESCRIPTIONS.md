# TimeKind - Localized App Store Descriptions

## English (US/International)

### App Store Title (30 characters max)
TimeKind: Routine Buddy

### Subtitle (30 characters max)
Build Better Time Habits

### Short Description (80 characters max)
Track tasks. Understand your time. Build better habits with TimeKind.

### Full Description (4000 characters)
**TimeKind** is your personal routine buddy for building better time habits. Track how long tasks actually take, understand your patterns, and improve your time perception over time.

**Why TimeKind?**
Most people underestimate how long tasks take. TimeKind helps you bridge that gap by tracking actual vs. estimated time, revealing patterns in your work, and building your time awareness muscle.

**Key Features:**
- **Simple Timer** - One-tap task tracking with instant feedback
- **Smart Insights** - See your accuracy trends, best times of day, and category patterns
- **Task Templates** - Create reusable templates for recurring workflows
- **Journal** - Reflect on completed tasks and learn from your estimates
- **Heatmap** - Visualize your productivity patterns across weeks and months
- **Offline First** - All data stays on your device, no cloud required
- **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes, day or night
- **Accessibility** - Built for neurodivergent users with customizable UI

**Perfect For:**
- Developers and knowledge workers
- Students and academics
- Freelancers and entrepreneurs
- Anyone wanting to understand their time better

**Privacy First:**
Your data never leaves your device. TimeKind is completely offline and doesn't require an account. Optional analytics help us improve the app while respecting your privacy.

**Get Started:**
Download TimeKind today and start building better time habits. Your first insight is just a task away.

### Keywords
time tracking, productivity, habits, routine, task management, time awareness, neurodivergent, ADHD, focus, time estimation
