## iOS App Store Requirements

**Screenshot Specifications:**
- **Dimensions:** 1242×2208 pixels (required for iPhone)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Quantity:** 2-10 screenshots (recommended: 5-6)
- **Safe area:** Keep important content within center 1080×1920 area
- **Text overlay:** Optional (can add captions for context)

**Upload Process:**
1. Go to App Store Connect
2. Navigate to your app → App Store tab
3. Scroll to "Preview and Screenshots"
4. Select device type (iPhone 6.7-inch or iPhone 5.5-inch)
5. Upload screenshots in order
6. Add optional descriptions for each screenshot

**Best Practices:**
- Show real app interface (no mockups)
- Highlight unique features in first 2-3 screenshots
- Use consistent theme (light or dark mode)
- Keep text minimal and readable
- Show actual data (tasks, entries, charts)

## Google Play Store Requirements

**Screenshot Specifications:**
- **Dimensions:** 1080×1920 pixels (required for phones)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Quantity:** 2-8 screenshots (recommended: 4-6)
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16
- **File size:** Max 8 MB per image

**Upload Process:**
1. Go to Google Play Console
2. Navigate to your app → Store listing
3. Scroll to "Screenshots"
4. Click "Add screenshot"
5. Upload up to 8 screenshots
6. Add optional descriptions

**Best Practices:**
- Show app in portrait orientation
- Include UI elements (status bar, navigation)
- Highlight different features across screenshots
- Use consistent color scheme
- Add 1-2 lines of descriptive text per screenshot
